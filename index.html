Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

A QR code is a type of barcode that stores information in a two-dimensional space. It can be scanned using smartphones to quickly access URLs, text, and more.

Why are QR codes used?

QR codes are used for quick access to information such as websites, contact details, and locations. They are highly efficient for marketing, inventory, and identification purposes.

How do I create a QR code?

To create a QR code, simply enter the information you want to encode (e.g., a URL, text, or contact information) in the generator, customize the look if desired, and then click "Generate." A QR code will be created instantly.

How do I scan a QR code?

To scan a QR code, simply open the camera on your smartphone (or use a QR code scanning app). Point the camera at the QR code, and a notification or link will appear for you to tap and view the encoded information.

What can I store in a QR code?

QR codes can store various types of data, including:

  • Website URLs
  • Contact information (vCards)
  • Plain text
  • Wi-Fi network details
  • Email addresses
  • Location coordinates
  • Event details (calendar invites)
  • Social media links
